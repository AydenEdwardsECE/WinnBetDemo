What is sports betting?

At a glance: placing a wager on the outcome or statistics of a sporting event. Common markets include moneylines (who wins), spreads (win margin), and totals (combined points). More advanced markets involve props and live, in‑play bets.

How it works

You select a market at listed odds. If your outcome occurs, you’re paid according to those odds; otherwise, you lose your stake. Odds reflect implied probability and operator margin. Play should always be discretionary and within set limits.

Key concepts

Bankroll (your set budget), unit sizing (small, consistent stakes), and variance (short‑term randomness) help keep expectations realistic. Our Advisory/Set Modes help reinforce these habits.

Frequently asked questions

Answers to common questions about sports betting, accounts, and responsible play.

Who can place a bet?

You must be 21+ and physically located in a state where mobile sports betting is legal at the time of wagering. Geolocation is required to verify location.

How do odds work (moneyline, spread, totals)?

Moneyline is who wins outright. Spread adds or subtracts points from a team’s final score. Totals are combined points by both teams. Odds reflect implied probability and house margin.

What are deposits, withdrawals, and limits?

We support common payment methods. You can set deposit, wager, and time limits at any time. Payout times vary by method and may require verification.

Do you offer tools to help me stay in control?

Yes: optional Advisory Mode, Set Mode (hard limits), cool‑off periods, reality checks, and self‑exclusion. See Responsible Play for details.

Why do I need to enable location services?

State regulations require us to confirm you’re within a legal jurisdiction. We use certified geolocation providers to comply with the law.

How are promotions and bonuses handled?

Promotions have eligibility and play‑through requirements. We display plain‑English terms before you opt in.

Are winnings taxable?

In the U.S., gambling winnings are taxable income. We provide year‑end tax forms where applicable; consult a tax professional for guidance.

Where can I get help if I’m worried about my play?

Call or text 1‑800‑GAMBLER for confidential support, 24/7. You can also use in‑app tools or request self‑exclusion.
